Simple Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 16 oz spaghetti
  • 4 oz pecorino-romano cheese(make sure it is cubed)
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 2 cups of loosely packed baby spinach (make sure to only use the leaves)
  • 1 cup of firmly packed basil (make sure to only use the leaves)
  • 1/2 cup of walnuts that are chopped
  • 1/4 cup of lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp of lemon zest
  • 1/4 tsp of salt
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil

Steps:

  1. Bring pot of slightly salted water to boil
  2. Cook spaghetti at a boil until its cooked through but has a firm bite (this should take 12 minutes)
  3. Drain and transfer the spaghetti to a large bowl
  4. Put the Pecorino Romano cheese in a food processor grind for about 30 seconds
  5. Put the garlic in with the cheese and process it until the two are combined
  6. Add the spinach, basil, walnuts, lemon juice and zest, and salt with the garlic and cheese and grind until the mixture is a thick paste
  7. Scrape the mixture down the sides with a spatula
  8. Add olive oil to the mixture while the food processor is running and continue until the oil is smoothly integrated
  9. Pour the sauce over the spaghetti, toss to coat it, and enjoy!
