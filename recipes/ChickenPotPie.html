Simple Chicken Pot Pie Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole roasted chicken
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 yellow onion diced
  • 3 carrots cubed
  • 3 ribs celery cut in 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp chicken bouillon paste
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1 lb dry pasta shells

Crumb Topping

  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

Steps:

  1. Pull meat from chicken & refrigerate till needed
  2. Add 2 quarts of water to a pot and add chicken bones, skin,any meat scraps, and bay leaf.
  3. First bring the stock to a simmer on high heat
  4. Then reduce to low and let it simmer for 2 hours
  5. Remove to cool for about 1 hour
  6. Strain the broth and put it in the refrigerator until needed (this should make more than 6 cups)
  7. Add butter to a pot and melt over high heat
  8. Add onions carrots, celery, and salt and then cook it until the onions are translucent (should take 5 minutes)
  9. Season with pepper, thyme, garlic powder, and cayenne and cook for 1 more minute
  10. Add the flour and cook it until the flour becomes golden brown which should take 3-4 minutes
  11. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F or 190 degrees C
  12. Fill a larger pot with slightly salted water and bring it to a boil
  13. Stir in pasta and bring it back to a boil
  14. Cook the pasta uncovered stirring occasionally for a minute less than on a package directions
  15. Drain the pasta and stir it into the pot pie mixture
  16. Transfer everything into a large deep baking dish which should be about 4.5 quarts and distribute evenly
  17. Stir the panko, salt, and melted butter together in a bowl and sprinkle it over the casserole
  18. Bake it in the preheated oven until the casserole is bubbling and the top is browned (should take about 45-60 minutes)
  19. Let it rest 10-15 minutes before serving
